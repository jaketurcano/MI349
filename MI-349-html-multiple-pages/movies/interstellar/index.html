Interstellar

Another one of my favorite movies is the sience fiction film Interstellar (2014). This movie is about a father that must go on a mission through space to find another habitable world since the earth is dying. He has to leave his family behind to save them and the rest of the earth. I really enjoyed this movie's story as it is very emotional and thought provoking. However, my favorite thing about this movie is the visuals and music that accompany it. It is a very intense and moving piece.
