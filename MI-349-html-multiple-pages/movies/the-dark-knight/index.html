The Dark Knight

My other favorite movie is the super hero movie The Dark Knight (2008). This is a sequel to the movie Batman Begins, which both are coincidentally directed by Christopher Nolan who also directed Interstellar. This movie introduces us to the Joker as the villain who tries to create chaos in Gotham. Batman fights to stop him while saving citizens and trying to keep his friends safe. This is one of my all time favorite movies for many reasons. Batman is my favorite comic book character and Joker is one of my favorite villains. I think Heath Ledger played an amazing Joker, and everything between the acting, set designs, music, and special effects were incredible.
