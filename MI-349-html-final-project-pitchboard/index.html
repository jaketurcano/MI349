Student Portfolio Website

The Student Portfolio Website is a template for all those who seek to promote themselevs and their work by publicly displaying and sharing it with peers and job recruiters.

Following graduation, many students do not know the best action to take for finding a job that will best suit them. Part of this issue comes from the lacking knowledge of how to market oneself besides a resume and possibly a linkedin page. Despite this, companies still search for a stand-out factor in deciding between candidates. This means every piece you market yourself with should better your chances at finding a job, or at least standing out to recruiters. One method of presenting yourself in a different way is by displaying all of your professional goals and acheivments on an accessible website. Doing this makes it easier to find information on potential job candidates as well as simplifying sharing resources or contact information. However, this prime example of marketing yourself can only be useful if you have a prior knowledge of web development and its subsequent frameworks. That is why I propose making a general web template for students to display whatever information about them they want in order to build a personal portfolio.
